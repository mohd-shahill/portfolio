How to add a custom profile photo to the About Me window:

1. Place your image inside this folder (public/profile/).
2. Name it "profile.jpg", "profile.png", "profile.jpeg", or "profile.webp".
3. Refresh the page!

The system will automatically detect the image and replace the "MS" initials with your custom photo.
It is recommended to use a square image (1:1 aspect ratio) for best results.
