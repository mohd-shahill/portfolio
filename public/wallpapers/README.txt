HOW TO SET YOUR WALLPAPER
══════════════════════════════════════════

1. Drop any photo into this folder.
2. Rename it to exactly:  wallpaper.jpg
   (or wallpaper.png / wallpaper.webp)
3. Save the file, then refresh your browser.

Done! Your photo will auto-appear as the desktop background.

TIPS:
 • Best resolution: 1920×1080 or higher
 • Landscape orientation looks best
 • Supported formats: .jpg  .jpeg  .png  .webp
